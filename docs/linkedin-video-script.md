# Sleep-Scribe — LinkedIn Video Script (~60 seconds)

Keep this open on a second screen (or phone) while recording.
Rule: record screen footage first, then record the voiceover over it.

---

## SHOT 1 — Hook (0:00–0:07)
**Show:** Landing page. Slow scroll through the particle galaxy hero.

> "Dreams fade within five minutes of waking. So I built Sleep-Scribe —
> an AI sleep journal that catches them first, and tells you what they mean."

## SHOT 2 — Journal + AI analysis (0:07–0:22)
**Show:** Journal page. Paste a pre-written dream (have it in clipboard!),
hit analyze, let the result appear — mood, symbols, themes, tip.

> "You capture a dream in seconds — voice or text. Then a real LLM,
> Llama 3.3 running on Groq, breaks it down: the mood, the symbols,
> the hidden themes — and gives you a plain-language interpretation."

## SHOT 3 — Luna, the AI companion (0:22–0:32)
**Show:** Click the floating chat bubble → click a suggestion chip
("What patterns do you notice in my journal?") → let the answer appear.

> "This is Luna — and she's not a generic chatbot. She reads your own
> dream journal. Ask her what symbols keep recurring, or how your mood
> has changed — she answers from your data, not generic advice."

## SHOT 4 — Insights (0:32–0:40)
**Show:** Quick cuts — Dashboard weekly chart → Insights mood breakdown
→ common symbols. 3 seconds each, don't linger.

> "Over time it finds the patterns behind your good and bad nights —
> sleep quality, mood correlation, recurring symbols. Plus streaks,
> smart wind-down tips, and full data export."

## SHOT 5 — Tech stack (0:40–0:52)
**Show:** Quick flash of VS Code / README, then MongoDB Atlas dashboard.

> "Under the hood: React and TypeScript on the frontend with a Three.js
> particle galaxy. Node and Express on the backend, MongoDB Atlas for the
> database, JWT and Google auth — and the AI runs server-side, so the API
> key never touches the browser. All deployed on Vercel. Built in five
> days for the Conesta Forge internship."

## SHOT 6 — Close (0:52–0:58)
**Show:** Back to the galaxy hero, or the dashboard.

> "Link's in the post — try it, and tell me what your dreams say about you."

---

## Recording checklist
- [ ] Pre-seed the app with several journal entries (Dashboard/Insights must not look empty)
- [ ] Dream text copied to clipboard before recording
- [ ] Browser full-screen, bookmarks bar hidden, 100% zoom
- [ ] Record screen first, voiceover after
- [ ] Cut every dead moment
- [ ] Add captions/subtitles — most people watch muted
